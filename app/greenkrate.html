GreenKrate App

GreenKrate is a responsive web application that provides weekly delivery of household goods & services at everyday retail prices.

EmberJS • Javascript • Firebase • Braintree • Bootstrap • OAuth • Google Cloud • Heroku • NodeJS • Express


The Client Goal

GreenKrate sought to develop a mobile-friendly web application that redefined the online grocery shopping experience. Their customers range from busy households to urban professionals and business looking to reduce the four-hour grocery shopping process.

Focused on delivering value through convenience and quality, they wanted both an administrative dashboard and customer facing store-front. They tasked Makapen with the design and development their MVP.

What We Did

We developed an e-commerce platform that integrates social media and payment processing within a storefront marketplace. This was achieved by architecting a front and back end which scaled to the changing requirements throughout the development process.

The GreenKrate platform uses Google cloud solutions for the infrastructure, Braintree for processing payments, OAuth for social media integration, EmberJS, Javascript and Bootstrap for the UX/UI, NodeJS and express for the middleware and Firebase for the backend and storing of real-time data.

The Result

As a result of our agile philosophy, we gathered customer feedback throughout the development lifecycle which made significant impacts to the accessibility of the user interface and overall software design. Today, GreenKrate customers enjoy the ease grocery shopping on any device through our responsive design. We continue to work with the GreenKrate team as they build new features and support their existing project.
